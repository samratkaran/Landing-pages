OVERVIEW

The International Institute of Business Studies (IIBS) is a private educational institution located in Bangalore, India. The institute was established in 1998 and since then it has been providing quality business management education to students. The institute offers a two-year full-time MBA and PGDM program, approved by AICTE and affiliated with Bengaluru City University. They also offer various certification programs in collaboration with prestigious institutions like IIM Bangalore. The institute fosters a dynamic learning environment that encourages critical thinking, innovation, and collaboration.
