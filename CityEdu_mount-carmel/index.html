OVERVIEW

Mount Carmel College (MCC) is a prestigious women's college in bangalore. It was established in 1948, and is affiliated to Bangalore University. The college is known for its quality education for Arts, Science, and Commerce streams. MCC offers UG, PG and Doctoral Programs in various disciplines such as arts, science, commerce, business management, computer applications, and vocational courses. Various companies visit the campus for recruitment some of which are Myntra, Cisco, McKinsey, Cognisant, Wipro, Wells Fargo, EY, Deloitte, Barclays, TCS, Taj, The Hindu, Big basket etc.

Eligibility

Under-Graduate

Students who have completed their II PUC/ 12th Standard of CBSE/ISC or any other recognized board in any stream (Arts, Science or Commerce) are eligible for admission to the program.

Post-Graduate

Passed in the respective qualifying UG examination with an aggregate of 50% marks in the core subject is the eligibility criteria for all the PG programs.
