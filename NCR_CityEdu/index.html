MBA Admission Process in Bangalore

The MBA admission process typically involves the following steps:

Application Submission: Candidates need to submit their applications with the required documents, academic transcripts, and entrance exam scores.
Entrance Exam Scores: Most MBA programs require valid scores from recognized entrance exams like CAT, GMAT, or GRE.
Personal Interview/GD: Shortlisted candidates are invited for a personal interview or group discussion to assess their communication skills, subject knowledge, and suitability for the program.
