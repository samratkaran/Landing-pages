Overview

ISME Bangalore was established in 2006. It is a leading business school that offers world-class education through a student-focused culture of excellence, international outlook, entrepreneurial thinking & industry alignment. ISME is part of an ISO accreditation body NVT Group, that has certified companies for over 25 years across 36 countries. The College has been ranked as one of the top B-Schools in India with all faculty having global & corporate exposure. Over 1700 ISME alumni are working in multinationals across the world. ISME aims at providing 100% placement for its students. Every year many companies visit the ISME campus placement drive such as Nielsen, Amazon, Asian Paints, Aditya Birla Group, Capillary Technologies, Deloitte, Café Coffee Day, HCL, Myntra and many more.

Eligibility

Under-Graduate

The students should have passed their PUC or Class 12th in any stream with 50% aggregate (for the general category) and 45% aggregate (for OBC/SC/ST category) from a recognised board.

Post-Graduate

The students should have passed their under-graduation degree in any stream with 50% aggregate (for general category) and 45% aggregate (for OBC/SC/ST category) from recognised universities

The students have to qualify any of the management entrance exams like CAT/MAT/CMAT/ATMA/GMAT/XAT/KMAT/PGCET to get MBA admission in ISME Bangalore.

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered at ISME?

A. The College offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various streams such as Commerce, and Management.

Does ISME offer hostel facilities?

Yes, ISME offers hostel facilities for its students. The college has four hostels that can accommodate over 1500 students. The hostel rooms are spacious, well-furnished, and equipped with all the necessary amenities.

What are the placement opportunities at ISME?

The College has an active placement cell that facilitates the placement of students in reputed companies. The cell also organises workshops, seminars, and training programs to enhance the employability skills of students. Some of the top recruiters at the college include Deloitte, EY, KPMG, Amazon, and Wipro, among others.

Is ISME UGC recognized?

Yes, ISME is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), which is the national regulatory body for higher education in India.
