OVERVIEW

GD Goenka University is a private university located in Sohna, Gurgaon, Haryana, India. The university was established in 2013 and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses in various fields, including engineering, management, law, education, hospitality, architecture, and media and communication, among others. GD Goenka University has collaborations with various industries and organizations to provide students with practical exposure and industry-relevant skills. The university also offers internships and training programs to students to help them gain real-world experience.

PLACEMENTS

GD Goenka University has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in finding suitable job opportunities after completion of their degree programs. The university has collaborations with various reputed companies and organizations for internships and placements. Some of the top recruiters who have hired students from GD Goenka University include Amazon, Deloitte, IBM, HDFC Bank, TCS, Accenture, HCL Technologies, Wipro, and many more. The university has an impressive placement record with over 500 companies visiting the campus for recruitment every year. The placement cell conducts various activities, such as industry-academia interface, guest lectures, mock interviews, resume building workshops, and personality development programs to enhance the employability skills of students.
