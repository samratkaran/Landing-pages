OVERVIEW

Christ University Bangalore, established in 1969 is one of the most sought-after institutes in the country. Accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC and NBA, it provides various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Management, Law, Science, and other fields. Notable personalities like Dalai Lama and Sudha Murty have visited the campus, which hosts over 520 international students and organizes conferences and seminars. It has three campuses in Bengaluru and is approved by UGC, BCI, AICTE, and NCTE.
