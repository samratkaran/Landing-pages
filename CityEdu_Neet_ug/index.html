NEET UG 2024

NEET-UG is the single medical entrance examination conducted for admission to undergraduate medical education in India. Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Nearly 16 lakh aspirants appear for NEET UG each year competing for admission into more than 80,000 seats across the country. Based on a standardized centralized syllabus and curriculum, NEET ranks students on an equitable scorecard for allocation into medical/dental colleges through a centralized counseling process.

While 15% of seats fall under the All India Quota filled solely by NEET merit ranks, states filled the remaining seats based on score and their reservation policies.

Application Process

  • Step 1: Visit the official NEET website: https://neet.nta.nic.in/ and Click on "New Registration" under "NEET (UG)".
  • Step 2: Enter your details like name, email, mobile number, and educational qualifications.
  • Step 3: Log in to the NEET website using your application number and password.
  • Step 4: Fill in all the required details like academic details.
  • Step 5: Pay the fee online using a credit/debit card, net banking, or UPI.
  • Step 6: Final Submission and Confirmation.
  • Step 7: Review your application form thoroughly for any errors or missing information. Click on the "Submit" button to finalize your application.
  • Step 8: Print and save a copy of the confirmation page for future reference.
