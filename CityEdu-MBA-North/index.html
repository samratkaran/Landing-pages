Why study in Delhi NCR

  • Leading in career advancement opportunities: Delhi NCR has emerged as a thriving IT & corporate development city. Cities like Gurugram, Noida, Sohna, etc have some of the leading global IT giants and heavy industries.
  • India’s Top-Notch B-Schools are here: Delhi NCR not only leads in industrialization but is also the home for India’s most prestigious B-Schools like Amity University, IILM University, Sharda University, Bennett University, and more
  • Affordable Education & lifestyle: Compared to other high-tech corporate cities in India, Delhi NCR offers a much more affordable yet high living standard and lifestyle.
  • A hub inspiring entrepreneurship: Every year the highest number of patents are filed with thousands of new companies getting registered, Delhi NCR inspires every bit of entrepreneurship in individuals.
