VITEEE overview

Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Exam (VITEEE), is the entrance exam conducted by Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) to select students for admission into various undergraduate engineering programs offered at VIT campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Amaravati, and Bhopal. Around 2 lakh students appear for the exam every year for a limited number of seats. Based on VITEEE ranks and preference, students are allotted seats across various engineering branches like CSE, Mechanical, Civil, etc offered at VIT campuses.

Why you should take VITEEE

  • Gateway to Leading Engineering Institution: VIT is a pioneering and leading institution known for quality technical education in India.
  • Multi-Campus Options: Qualifying in VITEEE gives you admission eligibility across 4 campuses - Vellore, Chennai, AP and Bhopal, so more options to choose from.
  • Well-organized Exam and Counselling: The entrance exam and subsequent counseling process are transparent and conveniently organized.
  • Facilitates Scholarships: Good ranks in VITEEE enable you to apply for attractive merit-based scholarships offered by VIT.
  • Pathway to Higher Studies: Along with B.Tech, integrated M.Tech & B.Tech+M.Tech dual degrees are also available through VITEEE to facilitate continuation for PG.
  • International Opportunities: VIT has global tie-ups which allow the possibility to spend a semester abroad or pursue a dual degree pathway.

Eligibility Criteria

10+2 passed students with a minimum aggregate of 60% (50% for SC/ST/OBC) marks in Physics, Chemistry/Biology, Mathematics, and English as a compulsory subject.

Applicants must be born on or after July 01, 2002.

Syllabus

The VITEEE syllabus includes 11th and 12th CBSE Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics syllabi as NCERT prescribes. Questions from the Biology and Aptitude section may not align directly with any textbook.

FAQs about VITEEE

VITEEE or Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Exam is a national-level entrance test for admission to B.Tech programs offered at VIT campuses.

Candidates should have passed Class 12th or equivalent qualification in the relevant stream with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks in PCM subjects..

You can apply online at vit.ac.in. Fill out the registration form, upload documents, and pay the application fee online via net banking/debit card/credit card.

There is no limit on the number of attempts for VITEEE. You can reappear to improve your score.

The application fee for VITEEE 2024 is Rs. 1,350. Additional charges apply for applications outside India.

Documents required include - 10th/12th mark sheets, DOB proof, medical certificate, community certificate (if applicable), passport photos, etc.

VITEEE admit cards will be issued in 4th week of March 2024. Download from the application portal using registration credentials.
