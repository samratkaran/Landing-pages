OVERVIEW BMSCE BANGALORE

Founded in 1946, it is the first private engineering college in India. BMS College of Engineering (popularly known as BMSCE) is located in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It is the first Engineering college in Karnataka to be accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Currently, BMS College of Engineering offers UG and PG courses in conventional and emerging areas. 250+ Companies from various sectors participated in the placement drive in 2023 to hire students at BMSCE
