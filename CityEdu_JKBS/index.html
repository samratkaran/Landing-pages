OVERVIEW

JK Business School (JKBS) was established in 2006, under the aegis of India’s most prominent business leader Shri Gobind Hari Singhania, the family-owning JK Organization. Located in the tech city, JKBS Gurgaon has multiple recognitions for its flagship AICTE-approved and industry-oriented PGDM programs. The institute also offers UG courses affiliated to the Gurugram University.

JKBS Gurugram Fees Structure

JKBS Gurgaon PGDM fee is Rs 7,99,000 in total to be paid in 4 instalments. The total fees for BCA is INR 3,80,000 and for BBA, the total academic fee is Rs. 3,36,500 which excludes hostel, accommodation and miscellaneous fees.
