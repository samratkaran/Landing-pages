    Frequently Asked Questions

    IFIM College affiliated to?

    IFIM Bangalore was established in 2009 and is affiliated with Bangalore University. IFIM College is accredited with A Grade by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) and is approved by UGC and AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education).

    Is IFIM College a private college?

    Yes, IFIM College is a private autonomous college.

    How is the infrastructure of the IFIM College?

    IFIM College is spread across 2 acre of land. The college has a library, digital classrooms, IT and electronics labs, a psychology lab, an amphitheatre, an auditorium, canteen, and offers other facilities to students.

    Which scholarships are offered by IFIM College?

    Institute of Finance and International Management (IFIM Bangalore) offers scholarships to students as financial assistance. Institute of Finance and International Management (IFIM College) offers 100% scholarships to students.

    How are the placements at the IFIM College?

    IFIM College offers placement assistance to students. The top companies taking part in the placement drive at the IFIM College are Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and others. The highest package offered during 2022 placements was INR 15 LPA, the average package was INR 7.5 LPA, the placement percentage was 96%, and others.
