KCET Exam 2024

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) is the gateway to pursuing higher education in professional courses across prestigious institutes of Karnataka. Conducted annually by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA), KCET offers admission into engineering, pharmacy, agriculture, veterinary sciences, and other programs at the undergraduate level. KCET is a highly competitive exam taken by thousands of students each year. With the right preparation strategy and guidance, you can improve your chances of getting into your desired college and course.

Key Features & Benefits of KCET

  • KCET offers students admission to top colleges in more than 150+ engineering, architectural, and agricultural institutes connected to Karnataka universities.
  • Centralized counseling process after the exam, where students can choose their preferred colleges and courses.
  • Compared to taking individual entrance exams for different colleges, KCET is a more cost-effective option for students.
  • KCET eliminates the need to take separate entrance exams for different colleges, saving students time and effort.
  • The scores can be used for lateral entry into some engineering colleges .

kcet Exam Pattern

The KCET 2024 will be a 3-hour offline pen-paper test, consisting of 240 MCQs from physics, chemistry, maths, and biology carrying 1 mark each, based on the 2nd PUC syllabus. Candidates can take the test in English, Hindi, Kannada, Marathi or Telugu. The total mark is 240 with no negative marking.

kcet Registration Process

Registration Process

  • Step 1: Visit the KCET official website
  • Step 2: Click on the “New User Registration 2024”
  • Step 3: Fill the required information and submit button
  • Step 4: After successful registration, a candidate will receive the KCET 2024 application number and User ID.
  • Step 5: Login again fill in the required details and upload the required documents.
  • Step 6: Now pay the application fee and take a printout of the receipt and application form.

Fees & Payment:

  • General and OBC candidates from Karnataka: INR 500
  • SC/ST and female candidates from Karnataka: INR 250
  • Candidates from outside Karnataka: INR 750
  • Applicants outside India: INR 5,000
