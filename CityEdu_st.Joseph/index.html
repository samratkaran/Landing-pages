OVERVIEW

St. Joseph's College of Commerce, located in Bangalore, Karnataka, is an autonomous private institution operated by the Jesuits of Karnataka. It was established in 1882 to provide education primarily to the minority Catholic community in the region. Affiliated with Bangalore University, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce, along with specialized courses. St. Joseph's College holds the esteemed status of "College for Excellence" (CE) by the UGC and has been accredited with an impressive A++ rating by NAAC.
