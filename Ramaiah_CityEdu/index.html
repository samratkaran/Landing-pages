OVERVIEW

M S Ramaiah College of Arts Sciences & Commerce was founded in 1994 and offers both UG and PG degrees in Arts, Science, and Commerce from Bangalore University. The college is authorized by AICTE in New Delhi and recognized by the Karnataka government. The college is also recognized by UGC and has been reaccredited with an "A" grade by the NAAC.

Passed 10+2 (Karnataka PUC / ISC / CBSE / NIOS / State Boards) from any recognized Board in India.
