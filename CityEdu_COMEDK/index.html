comedk 2024

COMEDK is a consortium of leading engineering colleges in Karnataka established in 1963. It is an autonomous body, which means the organization is solely organizing the examination without help from a government-led organization. Candidates interested in taking admission into Karnataka’s top institutions can appear in the COMEDK examination. It will help students get admission into more than 190+ private institutions in Karnataka.

COMEDK Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 passed/appearing students with a minimum aggregate of 45% marks (40% in respect of SC, ST, and OBC candidates of Karnataka State) in Physics, Chemistry/Biology/Bio-Technology, and Mathematics and English as a compulsory subject.
  • Students who want to pursue architecture programs must also qualify for the NATA examination

Exam Pattern

Computer-Based Test (CBT) with 3 sections: Physics (60 marks), Chemistry (60 marks), and Mathematics (60 marks). Total duration: 3 hours.
