nata Exam 2024

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA), conducted by the Council of Architecture (COA) since 2006, is an important requirement for admission to Bachelor of Architecture programs in many colleges across India. Admissions to B.Arch. programs are carried out by the CoA authorities of respective states, UTs, and institutions based on valid NATA scores and eligibility criteria set by them.

Important Highlights

  • Important assessments include- aptitude, cognitive skills, visual perception, aesthetic sensitivity, logical reasoning, critical thinking, and past learning.
  • NATA Exam will be conducted in multiple phases
  • Candidates can appear for one or all the exam phases
  • NATA test scores are valid for two academic years.

    Advantages of the NATA Exam

    • NATA simplifies the admission process by serving as a single entrance exam accepted by numerous reputable architectural colleges across India.
    • Successful completion of NATA and an architecture degree opens doors to diverse career opportunities in architectural firms, construction
    • companies, and urban planning departments.
    • With NATA, students can apply to multiple colleges using a single exam score, saving valuable time typically spent on registering and appearing for multiple entrance tests
